Data Source

This application obtains source data from the n3rgy consumer API. We make no guarantees regarding the accuracy or availability of this data.

Data Usage

The data displayed in this application should be considered estimates only. These estimates may differ from your actual energy costs as reflected on your official energy bill.

Purpose

The information provided by this application is for informational purposes only. It should not be relied upon as the sole basis for decision-making regarding your energy consumption or related financial decisions.

Local Data Storage

All data downloaded through this application is stored locally on your device. We do not store or process any of your energy consumption data on remote servers or machines.

Data Access

You agree to only use this application to view data for which you are the bill payer or have explicit permission from the bill payer to access.

Device Usage

For your privacy and data security, you should only use this application on your own private device. Using the application on public devices may expose your data to unauthorized access.

Limitations

While we strive to provide useful information, this application makes no guarantees as to the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of the data presented. Users should be aware of these limitations when interpreting the information provided.

Updates

We reserve the right to update this statement as necessary. Please review it periodically for any changes.
